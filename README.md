# 📊 Customer & Sales Analytics Dashboard Project

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, sales performance, and financial metrics to generate meaningful business insights using data analytics techniques.

It includes both **Sales Analytics** and **Finance Analytics** dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

### 1. Customer Performance Report
- Analyze customer purchase behavior and contribution to revenue
- Identify high-value and low-value customers

### 2. Sales Performance vs Target Analysis
- Compare actual sales performance against business targets
- Identify gaps and improvement opportunities

---

## 📌 Purpose of Sales Analytics

Sales analytics helps businesses monitor, evaluate, and improve sales performance by converting raw data into actionable insights.

---

## 📈 Importance of Sales Data Analysis

- Identify sales trends and seasonal patterns  
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs)  
- Understand product and customer performance  
- Enable data-driven decision-making  

---

## 🧠 Role of Sales Reports

- Identify effective discount strategies  
- Support customer negotiation strategies  
- Discover business expansion opportunities  
- Highlight high-performing markets and customers  

---

## 💰 Finance Report

### 🎯 Project Objectives

#### 1. Profit & Loss (P&L) Report by Fiscal Year & Months
- Evaluate financial performance over time
- Track revenue, cost, and profit trends

#### 2. Profit & Loss (P&L) Report by Markets
- Compare financial performance across different regions/markets

---

## 📌 Purpose of Finance Analytics

Finance analytics helps evaluate financial performance and supports strategic decision-making for business growth and stability.

---

## 📈 Importance of Financial Data Analysis

- Benchmark performance against previous periods  
- Compare performance across markets  
- Support budgeting and forecasting  
- Improve financial planning accuracy  

---

## 🧠 Role of Financial Reports

- Align financial planning with business strategy  
- Provide clarity on financial health  
- Build stakeholder confidence  
- Support long-term business growth decisions  

---
